Soup

Description

Soup is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Soup can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical soup dish which is a dish made with several layers of soup sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the soup noodles.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • No spit
